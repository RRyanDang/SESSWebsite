What is SEC?

The Seneca Engineering Competition (SEC) offers engineering students an opportunity to showcase their skills in innovation, design, and communication by tackling real-world challenges. Participants can choose from a range of challenges to compete in. Winners will advance to represent Seneca at the Ontario Engineering Competition, where they will compete against teams from other schools across the province.


The SEC has two challenges: Programming and Re-Engineering.
